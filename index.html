Hangman

Lives: 10

How to Play

  • 1. Choose a letter from the alphabet
  • 2. If the letter is in the word, choose another letter
  • 3. If the letter is not in the word, a portion of the hangman is added
  • 4. If you guess the word, you WIN! or if the hangman is complete, you LOSE!
